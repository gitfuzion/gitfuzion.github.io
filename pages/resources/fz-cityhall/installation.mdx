# FzCityhall

## Installation
1. Make sure you have installed all dependencies.

Required Dependencies:
- [ox_lib](https://github.com/overextended/ox_lib)
- [qb-inventory](https://github.com/qbcore-framework/qb-inventory) or [ox_inventory](https://github.com/overextended/ox_inventory)

Optional dependencies:
- [qb-target](https://github.com/qbcore-framework/qb-target) or [ox_target](https://github.com/overextended/ox_target)

2. Download the resource and place it in your `resources` directory.
3. Add `ensure fz-moneywash` to your `server.cfg` file.
4. Configure the resource to your liking.
5. Restart/Start your server.
6. That's it your done!
