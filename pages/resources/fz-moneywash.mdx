# FzMoneywash

## Dependencies
- qbx_core
- ox_inventory
- ox_lib

## Installation
1. Download the resource and place it in your `resources` directory.
2. Add `ensure fz-moneywash` to your `server.cfg` file.
3. Configure the resource to your liking.
4. Done, Its that easy!
